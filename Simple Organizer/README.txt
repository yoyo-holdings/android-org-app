SIMPLE ORGANIZER

Author: Patrich Paolo B. Datu
Date Created: April 9, 2014

This application is composed of two views:
Notes View and To do View

Use the respective buttons at the bottom bar to navigate between Notes View and Todo View

Notes View
To add notes, tap on the 'Type a note...' bar, a new note will be added where you can edit. 
To save the note, tap on the check button at the top right part of the note.
A more button is also available to update and delete notes.

Todo View
Add todo items through the "Add to do" field and press "ADD".
On this view, your may freely modify any todo item, it will be saved automatically.
Click on the delete button on the right of the todo item to delete the note.
